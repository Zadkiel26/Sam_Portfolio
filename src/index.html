About me

Samantha Figueroa

My name is Samantha Figueroa, I have always gone by Sam. I am a 24 year old born and raised in LA. I always had a curiosity for music, however I never really focused on it because I thought I had no talent since no one in my family is musically inclined. Once I had enough money saved up, I was able to get my first guitar. Therefore, I didn't start singing till the age of 13. I wrote my first song for my little sister, after she was diagnosed with cancer. This song was my way of telling her to stay strong which in the end helped her until she was finally free. Ever since then I have been writing and learning new instruments. These include the piano and ukulele. I also have been learning to produce and anything about music in general. The music I normally make sticks to the pop or singer/songwriter genre. I prefer to make music that is very low-key, simple and focuses on the artist rather than the music. I have dabbled in other genres though. For example. I was a feature for one of my friends song which was a EDM inspired beat. The last song I released was more pop/rock inspired beat, I wanted to try to get people to dance with that one. Aside from my family, music is one of the most important things in my life. Without it, I would not be here today. I think music is a form of art in which someone can tell a story without it having to make sense or even be long. I also feel like music is a way of telling someone how you feel without telling them.

3 Wishes

If I had three wishes

I’d waste them all on you

I’d wish to have you here with me

To have those nights where

We’d be watching your favorite movies

Eating junk food that you couldn’t have

Laughing every time I lost poker

and I’d be hugging you

I’d wish to still be able to talk to you

So I can tell you about a boy I’m seeing

Just to hear you call him names

Not writing this as my only form of communication

or having to visiting your grave when I miss you

I’d wish to visit you when I needed it the most

Like right before my marriage

When I buy my first house

Even when I have my first kids

Lastly, when it’s my time to go

If I had three wishes

They would all be spent on you

Stranger

I want to learn everything there is about you

I want to know every small detail like

the fact that one of your favorite colors is blue

and that the drops on roller coasters give you goosebumps

I want to explore every inch and curve on your body

only to find out you have a birthmark on the bottom of your left foot

and dimples on your cheek you are not fond of

I want to learn about all the things that make you happy like

the smell of popcorn at the movie theaters

and the sound of children's laughter

I want to tease you about the things that scare you the most like

heights or spiders and scuba diving

but more importantly losing loved ones

I want to hear about the things you hate like

glamping or scary movies

and the sound styrofoam makes when they clash together

I want you to drive me crazy with

the fact that you talk aloud in your sleep

and how you sing at the top of your lungs despite it being off key

I want to love you just as much as you love

sunsets on the beach

and rainy days because we get to watch movies all day long

I want you to tell me that you are who you are because

you’re the only male in the family of 4

and you love your mother more than anything else in the world

I want to know what you want in your future

like traveling the world

and starting a family of one boy, one girl, a dog and a cat

I want to learn everything about you

Until there’s nothing left

Until you’re no longer a stranger.

Glass

You broke me

Into twenty-one pieces

It was really easy for you

You always looked through me

You thought my fragility was beauty

You liked what you saw on the outside

But couldn’t see the scratches I had on the inside

Every piece shattered was a piece of my soul

But you didn’t care because you love to see things break
